# The Thatcher Family

A modest, hardworking household. In a town where everyone knows whose boots have been patched twice, the Thatchers are the family with the patched boots — and the quiet dignity to wear them without fuss. They are not wealthy, but they are loved.

---

## Thomas Thatcher — Gabriel's Father

✅ **[CANON]** Thomas is stoic and hardworking, with a strong, undemonstrative sense of duty. He gave Gabriel 65 coalmarks at the Night of Voices and told him: *"Don't blow it all on twine and clock parts"* — though with a twinkle in his eye. He loves his sons in the way of people who show it through showing up.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Thomas carries some private anxiety about Gabriel's pursuit of magic. He respects tradition and hard work, and the world of Spark feels uncertain to him. He won't oppose it — but he worries quietly.

---

## Elaina Thatcher — Gabriel's Mother

✅ **[CANON]** Elaina is practical and nurturing, gentle in her manner and often busy with the rhythms of the household. She fought alongside Gabriel and Jessica during the **Great Pumpkin Fray**, using the magic of her stone leaf to slow the Creeping Wither pumpkins, while Gabriel defended the streets with Poodler.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** *To be developed.* Elaina may have a deeper connection to the old traditions of Timberhearth than is immediately apparent. Leave room here.

---

## Orrin Thatcher — Gabriel's Younger Brother

✅ **[CANON]** Orrin is younger than Gabriel — younger enough that he watched his brother grow up as an observer, a step behind, wanting to belong. He has been secretly working with **Ringtail and the raccoons** for months, causing minor mischief (knocking over flowerpots, unstacking chairs, swapping bakery signs) to try to draw the town's attention to what was wrong. He revealed this at the Willow River gathering.

Orrin told Gabriel and Jessica: *"I follow them. Through alleys, over fences — they always lead me to him. The Ringtailed Ringleader. He doesn't talk, not like Whistlewing. But he shows me things. Things that don't make sense until later."*

He had also been visiting **Maribel Willowglen** in secret for weeks — bringing questions about raccoons and dreams, and sometimes just sitting and eating while she baked. She chose to believe him and kept his confidence.

**Orrin's nature:** He felt the spreading wrongness before anyone — the cracks in the air, the shadows in people's eyes. He acted alone and poorly, because he didn't know who to tell. Now he's no longer alone.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Orrin's bond with Ringtail is unusual. Even among the raccoon network, Ringtail chose Orrin specifically. There may be a reason for this that goes beyond Orrin being observant and nearby. His future role in the campaign may be significant.

---

## Poodler — The Thatcher Dog

✅ **[CANON]** Poodler is a white-furred, loyal, and deeply principled family dog. He fought with Gabriel and Jessica during the Great Pumpkin Fray. He led the way into Gnarltooth's tunnel beneath the village. He wore the magical vest.

✅ **[CANON]** When Gnarltooth's magical vest was fastened around him, Poodler was granted approximately **eight minutes of speech**. His priorities during this time were: (1) reporting that the scroll tasted like moldy socks and ambition, (2) confirming he did eat the bacon behind the stove, (3) expressing enthusiasm about possibly having arms, and (4) asking if squirrel cheese was a thing.

He spent the last of his time before the vest fizzled sitting on Gnarltooth's foot with a look of profound solidarity.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Gnarltooth said Poodler *"smelled like belonging."* This was not idle animal talk. There may be something about Poodler — his nature, his instincts, his quiet courage — that is connected to the old magic in ways no one yet understands.
