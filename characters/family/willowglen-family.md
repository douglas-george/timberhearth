# The Willowglen Family

The Willowglens run the bakery that fills the village square with the scent of honey rolls and sourdough every morning. They are prosperous by Timberhearth's standards — warm, generous, and quietly respected. The Willowglen Bakery is the kind of place where even a frown softens by the second roll.

---

## Maribel Willowglen — Jessica's Mother

✅ **[CANON]** Maribel is wise, empathetic, and unhurried. She listens the way good bread rises — without rushing, without judgment. She gave Jessica 250 coalmarks at the Night of Voices with the advice: *"Spend it wisely. But not too wisely. Life needs a little sweetness."*

✅ **[CANON]** When Gabriel and Jessica told her about the raccoons and the Ringtailed Ringleader, Maribel *gasped* — softly, but genuinely. She turned away just long enough to steady her expression. She knew.

She had been quietly meeting with **Orrin Thatcher** for weeks — Gabriel's younger brother had been coming to her kitchen door, quiet and hesitant, with questions about raccoons and dreams. She had chosen to believe him and kept his confidence. When Jessica confirmed what Orrin had described, Maribel understood that the thing she had quietly feared was real — but said only: *"I think something old is stirring. And I think you're part of the reason it's waking up."*

She did not tell them what she knew about Orrin. Not yet.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Maribel knows more than she has shared. She knows about Orrin. She may know older things too — connected to the bakery's history, the traditions of the town, perhaps even fragments of the old seal knowledge passed down in recipes, songs, or baking customs that carry more truth than anyone remembers. This is a thread worth developing.

---

## Bryna Willowglen — Jessica's Younger Sister

✅ **[CANON]** Bryna is 11 years old — playful, artistic, and full of sideways observations. She idolizes Jessica from a careful distance and has the social acuity of someone who has spent years reading the room to find her place in it.

During the Willow River gathering, Bryna insisted on carrying the jar of wildflower honey herself.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Bryna's curiosity and perceptiveness make her someone to watch. She notices things. She will start to notice more as the story progresses.

## The Willowglen Bakery

✅ **[CANON]** The Willowglen Bakery occupies a prime position on the village square and is the social heart of Timberhearth's mornings. It smells of honey bread, warm butter, and cinnamon. Maribel runs it with practiced, unhurried precision.

✅ **[CANON]** During Session 5, the bakery was **briefly overrun with every cat in Timberhearth** — a consequence of Poodler mishandling a sign. Maribel handled this with a rolling pin and a tight expression. The situation was resolved with Glowfern's help.

The bakery cats are now reduced to a manageable number. The orange one that guards the cash box may still be there.
