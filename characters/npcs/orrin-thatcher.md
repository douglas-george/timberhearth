# Orrin Thatcher — Gabriel's Younger Brother

---

## Who He Is

Orrin is Gabriel's younger brother — younger enough to have spent his early years as a step behind, watching, taking notes. He has the patient, slightly sideways intelligence of someone who learned to observe before he learned to act. He held a pumpkin seed in his palm for a whole afternoon once, staring at it as if it might whisper secrets. It didn't. But he was right to suspect it might.

---

## The Secret

✅ **[CANON]** For months before the campaign, Orrin was secretly recruited by **Ringtail** to help. Ringtail would send raccoons to tap at his window — bringing puzzles, leaving signs, leading him through alleys and over fences to meet the Ringleader. Ringtail showed him *things* — the cracks in the air, the spreading wrongness — and Orrin began to understand that something was wrong in Timberhearth.

To draw attention to the danger, Orrin caused minor mischief: knocking over flowerpots, unstacking chairs, swapping bakery signs. *"Nothing bad,"* he told Gabriel and Jessica. *"Just stuff to get attention. I thought maybe if people noticed something was weird, they'd start seeing the other things too."*

It mostly made people annoyed.

✅ **[CANON]** Orrin had also been visiting **Maribel Willowglen** in secret — coming to her kitchen door with questions about raccoons and dreams. She listened. She kept his confidence.

✅ **[CANON]** At the Willow River family gathering, sitting apart with his knees tucked to his chest and a river stone in his hand, Orrin finally told Gabriel and Jessica everything. *"I'm glad it's not just me anymore,"* he said.

---

## Current Status

✅ The secret is shared. Orrin is no longer carrying this alone. He is cautiously hopeful.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Ringtail chose Orrin deliberately. Among all the children in Timberhearth, it was Orrin he approached. There may be a reason rooted in something about Orrin's nature — an attunement, a connection, something latent. This is worth developing. What does Orrin have that Ringtail recognized?

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Orrin's relationship with Ringtail is not simply one of errand-boy and master. Something about how Ringtail communicated with him — *showing* rather than telling, through images and instinct — suggests that Orrin may be the key to reaching Ringtail again after the collapse at the pumpkin patch.
