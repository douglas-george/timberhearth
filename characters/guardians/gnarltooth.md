# Gnarltooth — The Mole Beneath the Town

---

## At a Glance

- **Animal form:** Massive mole — not tall, but wide, grounded, and solid. Chipped but strong claws. Amber eyes that glow faintly beneath a heavy brow. Fur matted with centuries of earth.
- **Human name:** Garron Holt — the gruff old miner of Hollowroot who hadn't come to a town feast in decades, but appeared at the Hollowroot Tree and stood firm without saying a word.
- **Role in the Covenant:** Descended into the soil to hold back the roots. Has been doing so ever since.
- **Location:** A chamber beneath Timberhearth, accessible through a rusted hatch in a clearing south of town (found by Poodler)

---

## Who Gnarltooth Is

Garron Holt was always more comfortable underground than above it. He showed up because it was right. He surrendered his form because someone had to. He has been alone in the tunnel for seven centuries, slow-carving runes into stone, holding the roots back, and thinking about what it would mean to surface.

*"I think about it all the time,"* he said.

He is massive, quiet, and unexpectedly gentle. He calls things as they are, without softness or cruelty. He speaks slowly and means everything he says.

---

## The Encounter

✅ **[CANON]** Poodler led Gabriel and Jessica to the rusted hatch and bolted inside without hesitation. Gnarltooth was in a wide chamber of glowing crystals and twisted root-lightning ceilings, carving something into stone with slow, careful movements.

*"You brought the loud one,"* he said, nodding at Poodler. *"That must mean it's serious."*

---

## The Magical Vest

✅ **[CANON]** Gnarltooth retrieved a small vest from a stone chest — stitched with silver thread, embroidered with runes, fitted with a glowing cravat. He gave it to them for Poodler. *"Put this on him. And be quick about it. The magic's got a time limit."*

It took two muffins, a piece of jerky, and Gabriel promising never to brush Poodler's fur again, but the vest was fastened.

Poodler received approximately **eight minutes of speech**. He used them well.

---

## The Quiet Admission

✅ **[CANON]** After the vest fizzled, Gnarltooth sat quietly. Jessica asked if he wanted to reconnect — with Timberhearth, with the other Guardians. He didn't answer at first.

Then: *"I think about it all the time."*

He had been awkward and too loud and too strange — and he chose to dig down instead of stand out. He thought he was helping. He thought maybe he was better off alone. *"But lately… the silence is louder than it used to be."*

Poodler rested his head on Gnarltooth's knee. *"You smell like belonging,"* he said quietly.

Gnarltooth nodded. *"Maybe… maybe you could take a message back. Just to start."*
