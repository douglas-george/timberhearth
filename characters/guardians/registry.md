# Guardian Registry — The Thirteen

Seven hundred twenty-two years ago, thirteen people surrendered their human forms to seal Vendraxis beneath Hollowroot Vale. They became the **Guardians Eternal** — neither beast nor mortal — bound to watch over the prison for as long as the seal holds.

Five have been found. Eight remain hidden.

## The Gifts and the Seal

👁️ **[REVEALED]** Gabriel's crystal and Jessica's stone leaf *resonate* with the Guardians. Whistlewing told them: *"Your gifts are part of the seal. They resonate with us. You will be drawn to those you are meant to find."*

The gifts act as a compass. They will pulse, hum, or warm in the presence of a Guardian — even a hidden one.
