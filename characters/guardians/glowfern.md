# Glowfern — Guardian of Poise, Keeper of Sunbeams

---

## At a Glance

- **Animal form:** Sleek silver cat — fur shimmering faintly as if dusted with starlight. Green eyes that follow every movement with calm authority. She organizes other cats through subtle tail-flicks and head-turns.
- **Human name:** Unknown. The Tome of Hollowroot Fragment does not record which of the thirteen Glowfern was before the Covenant.
- **Role in the Covenant:** Grace, balance, and precision. She moves through the world like someone who decided long ago that chaos was beneath her — and then manages it anyway.
- **Location:** The rooftops of Timberhearth. She knows where you are.

---

## Who Glowfern Is

Glowfern arrived among the bakery's accidental cat overflow and was immediately distinguishable: perfectly still on the windowsill while the others swung from bread racks and flour-bombed the mixing bowls. She organized them with nothing but presence. Then she looked at Jessica, blinked once, and walked out the back door.

She speaks directly into the mind — smooth, silky, and utterly self-assured.

*"You walk like unbalanced chairs."*

---

## The Three Trials

✅ **[CANON]**

**Trial One: The Balance of Grace** — She demanded they leap onto a wobbling barrel. Jessica managed. Gabriel spun into a crate of cabbages.

**Trial Two: The Patience of the Hunt** — She made them stand perfectly still beside a bowl for twenty minutes. The *"invisible mouse"* turned out to be a shimmer of magic that darted away. Poodler fell asleep upside-down.

**Trial Three: The Will to Serve** — She flopped onto her back and demanded brushing. Then summoned every kitten in the courtyard to join her. *"Yes,"* she murmured smugly. *"You'll do."*

---

## The Gift

✅ **[CANON]** Glowfern conjured a soft **silver whisker** from thin air and dropped it at Jessica's feet. *"A gift. Use it wisely — or humorously. Either is fine."*
