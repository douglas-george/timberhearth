# Session 02 — The Stump and the Shadows

## Summary

Seven days after the Night of Voices, Gabriel and Jessica set out to return to Whistlewing — only to be stopped by Mossel Crabtree, who had his own terms. A deal was struck, a stump was cleared, and magic lessons began. At nightfall, they investigated the bakery alley for the first time — and fled from glowing eyes in the dark.

---

## Events

### Mossel's Condition

Gabriel and Jessica crossed the square with lanterns in hand, heading for the tower. Mossel stepped into their path.

*"Not yet. There's a heap of storm-downed branches behind the schoolhouse — needs clearing before it smothers the lilacs. You do that for me, and I'll turn a blind eye when you slip past tonight."*

Gabriel winced. *"Another stump job?"*

*"Worse,"* Mossel said. *"It's wet."*

The work was done. The promise was kept. Mossel stepped aside.

### Second Visit to Whistlewing

Whistlewing was waiting. He told them he had kept watch and something had changed — shadows on rooftops, footsteps too soft to be a villager's, movement behind closed shutters. *"At first I thought it nothing more than raccoons. But no raccoon walks with one boot on and one boot off."*

He gave them a specific task: that night, after the square went still, walk the village. Start near the bakery. Observe only — do not confront. Return with what they found.

He also pointed them toward Shanna Parsnip — the Wisdom at the edge of town — to begin learning about magic. He told Jessica: *"Your spark grows brighter. It is not a thing you hold, but a thing you are."*

### Ivan Ranger's General Store

Gabriel purchased a **lantern** (50 coalmarks). Jessica purchased **lantern oil** (30 coalmarks). Ivan was distracted but kind.

### Shanna Parsnip

Shanna opened her door at the first silver touch of moonlight on the field outside her door — just as Whistlewing said she would. She introduced them to the concept of **Moonsparks** and the available cantrip-level spells: Prestidigitation, Windgust, and Whispercast.

### The Alley — First Attempt

Late that night: narrow alley behind the Willowglen Bakery, lantern light throwing long shadows across the cobblestones. They had followed animal tracks here from near the bakery.

Then: one pair of glowing eyes blinked open in the darkness.

Then another. Then ten more.

They fled.

---

## Key Choices Made

- Honored Mossel's deal and cleared the work before returning to the tower.
- Visited Shanna Parsnip to begin magic study.
- Investigated the alley — then retreated when confronted with the eyes.
