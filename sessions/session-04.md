# Session 04 — The Circle of Thirteen

## Summary

Gabriel and Jessica returned to Whistlewing with the news of Ringtail's collapse. The owl could no longer hope. He revealed the truth: thirteen former humans, seven centuries ago, gave up their human forms to seal Vendraxis. He is one of them. Their gifts resonate with the Guardians. They must find the others. Then they found the first: Mira the fox, in a woodland shrine slowly consumed by blight — who set them a trial, acknowledged them, and sent them back with a task still ahead.

---

## Events

### Whistlewing's Revelation

They climbed to the roost at three in the morning, hearts in their throats. Whistlewing looked up. He didn't ask what happened. He said: *"You saw it."*

When they told him Ringtail had collapsed, something shifted in him — feathers dulled at the edges. *"There's no more time for hoping."*

He drew aside a curtain. Behind it: a curved wall carved with thirteen sigils. One pulsing faintly with light.

*"There were thirteen Guardians. Each one gave up their old life to bind the Wither. Animal and human, spirit and sage. We were sealed to new forms, hidden from the enemy... and from ourselves."*

He explained the Covenant. He named himself — Elric Brightfeather. He named Ringtail — Kaelen of the Glades.

*"Some still remember. Others have forgotten who they are. Some may have been twisted by the blight. But we must find them all."*

He pointed at their gifts: *"Your gifts are part of the seal. They resonate with us. You will be drawn to those you are meant to find."*

*"There are eleven still hidden. Seek them in the woods, in the streets, in the places forgotten by time. Listen to those who speak in riddles. Watch those who walk alone. Trust your gifts."*

When Jessica asked about Ringtail: *"He will endure. Or he will not. But either way, the task remains."*

### The Tome of Hollowroot Fragment

In the days that followed, a fragment from an ancient text was encountered — a written record called the **Tome of Hollowroot**, dated Year 1 of the Quiet Age. It recorded, in full, the story of the Covenant of Binding: the thirteen people, their names, their animal forms, their sacrifice, and the pumpkin seed condition of the seal.

The players read it. The full truth of the thirteen Guardians became known to them: not just Whistlewing and Ringtail, but all thirteen names — Embermane, Stoneback, Mothshade, Hearthgnaw, Gnarltooth, Bramblegut, Mistpelt, Glintleap, Flicker, Skyflutter, Glowfern.

### Finding Embermane

Following instinct and the faint warm pulse of Jessica's stone leaf, Gabriel and Jessica made their way southeast through tangled underbrush — past vines that smelled faintly of rotting pumpkin — to a woodland shrine.

Stone arches curled with vines, half-sunken into moss. A ring of lilies, white and trembling. And some of them were turning dark at the tips.

They were not alone.

*"Oh my. What a rare and majestic creature wanders into my grove tonight."*

The voice arrived before the fox. A silver shape emerged from the shadows — graceful, gleaming, eyes like old winter.

*"You're Mira,"* Jessica breathed.

*"I am. Or I was. Or perhaps I will be again."*

She set them the **Trial of Balance**: a bridge of woven light and shadow across a void of writhing vines. One carried light, one carried shadow. They had to walk in perfect harmony.

They stumbled. They argued about which left. The bridge groaned. Then — hands clasped, breath matching, one step then another — they crossed together.

*"You kept balance,"* Mira said. *"Even in fear. Even in doubt. That is rare."*

But she could not leave. The blight had grown strong, and she alone held it back. *"You must return to me later, when the path is clearer."*

*"Next time? Leave the deer behind."*

*(Note: They had arrived in a homemade deer costume. Poodler accompanied them, barking the entire way. The costume had already lost a hoof.)*

## Key Choices Made

- Returned to Whistlewing immediately after Ringtail's collapse — did not wait.
- Engaged with the Tome of Hollowroot Fragment and absorbed the full history of the Covenant.
- Attempted Embermane's trial without hesitation.
