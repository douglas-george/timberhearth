# Vendraxis, the Creeping Wither

*Also known as: The Hollow Root, The Hunger Beneath*

---

## What the Players Know

👁️ **[REVEALED]** Vendraxis is a primordial being of decay and entropy. It is not driven by hatred or vengeance — it is driven by an unrelenting, mindless hunger to unravel life and reclaim it into silence. It predates Timberhearth. Seven hundred twenty-two years ago, it was sealed beneath the land by the thirteen Guardians at the cost of their human lives.

👁️ **[REVEALED]** Its influence spreads through **root systems, fungal spores, dying crops, and corrupted dreams**. It does not announce itself. It seeps. Plants wilt. Animals grow ill. Memories fade. As its power grows, physical manifestations appear: cursed vines, ambulatory pumpkins, toxic gas clouds, and runes etched into the soil by creeping roots.

👁️ **[REVEALED]** The seal is now failing. Vendraxis has begun to stir directly — the wild pumpkin patch, the in-town attack, the Great Pumpkin Fray. The blight has breached the village.

---

## The Pumpkin Connection

👁️ **[REVEALED]** The pumpkin seed ban is not folklore. **Pumpkin seeds are the keystone anchor of the prison's magical resonance.** Each seed consumed sends a ripple through the seal. As generations forgot, and more people began eating them again, the seal eroded. The wild pumpkin patch growing outside Timberhearth is the first visible sign. The in-town incursion was the second.

---

## Manifestations Encountered

✅ **[CANON]** The following have been witnessed by Gabriel and Jessica:

- The **corrupt pumpkin patch** at the edge of town: glowing pumpkins with rudimentary awareness, ambulatory vines, Ringtail's sigil mark on a scarecrow.
- **Ambulatory vines** capable of grabbing and lashing — one caught Gabriel's boot.
- The **Great Pumpkin Fray**: a coordinated incursion into the village square itself. Creeping Wither pumpkins attacked in the streets. The town fountain was destroyed.

---

## The Nature of Vendraxis

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Vendraxis does not speak or reason in mortal terms. It is alien. It does not want anything the way a villain wants things — it simply *undoes*. It feeds on excess, waste, overgrowth, and slothful magic left unattended. The pumpkin seeds are not a metaphor — they are literal fragments of Vendraxis's consciousness, distributed across every seed in the region. Each one eaten feeds him a tiny piece of waking.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** The physical manifestations are getting larger. The ambulatory vines will be followed by worse. Eventually, if the seal collapses entirely, Vendraxis will not merely influence — it will *manifest*. What that looks like, no living person has seen. The Guardians have only fragments.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Vendraxis cannot be destroyed. Only resealed. The question of how — and at what cost — is the campaign's central mystery.
