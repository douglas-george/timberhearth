# Magic — Spark and Moonspark

---

## What Magic Is

👁️ **[REVEALED]** Magic in Timberhearth is called **Spark** — or, in its refined and concentrated form, **Moonspark**. It is rare, real, and ancient. Magic is not an inherited bloodline gift. It is a *quality* in a person: something latent, shaped by character and intention, something to be cultivated rather than claimed.

Shanna Parsnip, the town's Wisdom, describes it this way:

> *"Magic isn't something you hold. It's something you are. You carry the breath of the old wild."*

---

## Moonsparks

✅ **[CANON]** A **Moonspark** is a concentrated unit of magical energy. One Moonspark can power a single cantrip-level spell. Moonsparks can be accumulated through practice and intent.

---

## Known Spells

✅ **[CANON]** The following cantrip-level spells have been learned or demonstrated:

**Prestidigitation** — Perform a small manipulation on a single object over a relatively short distance.

**Windgust** — Create a small but powerful gust of focused wind.

**Whispercast** — Send a seven-word message to anyone you know very well, anywhere in the world.

---

## The Players' Gifts

✅ **[CANON]** Both Gabriel and Jessica have been identified as Spark-touched:

- **Jessica** has a gift for **nature magic** — a deep affinity with growing things, living systems, roots, and rain. Her stone leaf from Whistlewing is the seed of this power.
- **Gabriel** has a gift for **connection magic** — bringing people, ideas, and broken things together. His heart-shaped crystal from Whistlewing is the seed of this power. It must first be developed *inward* before it can work *outward*.

---

## Shanna Parsnip

✅ **[CANON]** Shanna Parsnip is Timberhearth's **Wisdom** — the town's keeper of magical knowledge, living at the edge of the village where the vines grow over the fenceposts and the wind smells of mint and ash. She opened her door to Gabriel and Jessica at the first full moon after their Night of Voices ceremony.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Shanna knows more about the Guardians and the seal than she lets on. She is not one of the thirteen, but she carries old knowledge — passed down through a line of Wisdoms stretching back to the time of Hollowroot. She will share this when the players are ready for it.

---

## The Deeper Connection

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Magic in this region is intimately tied to the Guardians and the seal. As the seal weakens, ambient wild magic becomes less predictable — minor spells may flare unexpectedly, blight-touched plants may react strangely to nature magic. Jessica's nature affinity, in particular, will become more powerful and more dangerous as the seal erodes. This is both opportunity and warning.
