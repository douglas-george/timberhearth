# Timeline of Timberhearth

---

## The Deep Past

👁️ **[REVEALED]** — *Year 1 of the Quiet Age, 722 years ago*

The land was once called **Hollowroot Vale**. Vendraxis, the Creeping Wither, began to stir — pumpkin vines twisted, crops rotted, dreams turned wrong. Thirteen inhabitants of Hollowroot, led by **Elric Brightfeather** and **Kaelen of the Glades**, performed the **Covenant of Binding**: surrendering their human forms to become animal Guardians, sealing Vendraxis in a magical slumber beneath the earth. The condition of the seal: *no one may eat pumpkin seeds*. The town of Timberhearth grew up over the centuries, built above the vault without knowing it.

---

## The Long Middle

👁️ **[REVEALED]** — *Generations*

The truth of the seal was passed down, then distorted, then forgotten. Warnings became folklore. Folklore became superstition. Superstition became jokes. The Guardians endured, hidden in their animal forms, watching from the margins of the town they had once called home. The seal held — barely.

---

## Before the Campaign

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** — *Months before Session 1*

The pumpkin seed taboo has been quietly eroding. More townsfolk eat them, some openly. Vendraxis stirs more strongly with each passing season. Ringtail — Kaelen of the Glades in raccoon form — sensed the danger first and began **mobilizing raccoons** to cause minor mischief, trying to draw the town's attention to what was wrong. He recruited **Orrin Thatcher**, Gabriel's younger brother, as a secret collaborator.

Whistlewing, meanwhile, noticed unusual shadows and movements in Timberhearth — but did not yet understand that Ringtail was trying to help, not cause harm.

## Session Events (Canon)

✅ **[CANON]** — *Session 1: The Night of Voices*

Gabriel Thatcher and Jessica Willowglen are called to the clock tower **together** at the Night of Voices — the first time in Timberhearth's history that two youths have ever been summoned simultaneously. Whistlewing gives each a gift and asks them to investigate strange signs in the town.

✅ **[CANON]** — *Session 2: The Stump and the Shadows*

Mossel Crabtree stops them from returning to the tower until a stump is cleared. They honor the deal. Gabriel buys a lantern from Ivan Ranger (50 coalmarks). They visit Shanna Parsnip. They investigate footprints and have their first glimpse of glowing eyes in the alley — and flee.

✅ **[CANON]** — *Session 3: Secrets and Trust*

The players return to the alley and encounter the **Ringtailed Ringleader** with his synchronized raccoon troop. A rooftop human figure is also spotted. They choose to follow the raccoons. Ringtail burns a spiral sigil into the wall. Jessica presses bread to capture the mark. They confide in **Maribel Willowglen**, who knows more than she lets on. **Orrin** reveals his months-long secret collaboration with Ringtail. At the edge of town, they find the **corrupted pumpkin patch**. Ringtail intervenes at great personal cost, warning: *"The seal is failing."* He collapses.

✅ **[CANON]** — *Session 4: The Circle of Thirteen*

Whistlewing reveals the full truth: the thirteen Guardians, their sacrifices, their purpose. Gabriel and Jessica learn their gifts resonate with the Guardians and will guide them to those they are meant to find. They find **Mira (Embermane)** in her woodland shrine, pass her trial of balance, and are acknowledged as champions. She cannot yet leave her post. The **Tome of Hollowroot Fragment** (the full history of the Sealing and all Guardian names) is encountered.

✅ **[CANON]** — *Session 5: The Mole, the Cat, and the Fray*

Poodler leads them to a tunnel below the village where they meet **Gnarltooth** (Garron Holt), who has held the roots back underground for centuries. He gives Poodler a magical vest granting **eight minutes of speech**. Poodler makes excellent use of this. Gnarltooth asks for a message to be carried back — his first step toward reconnection. Through a sign accident, the Willowglen Bakery is overwhelmed with cats — and among them, **Glowfern**, the silver cat Guardian, who sets them three trials and gives Jessica a silver whisker. That same period: Vendraxis launches the **Great Pumpkin Fray**, a direct incursion into the village. Gabriel, Jessica, and Poodler drive back the Creeping Wither pumpkins. **The town fountain is destroyed.**

## Present — After Session 5

✅ **[CANON]** Five Guardians found: Whistlewing, Ringtail, Embermane, Gnarltooth, Glowfern.

🔒 **[HIDDEN]** Eight Guardians remain hidden. Ringtail's current state — alive, recovering, or worse — is unknown. The blight has now breached the village itself. The seal is fragile.
