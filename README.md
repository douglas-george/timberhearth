# 🌲 Timberhearth — Campaign Vault

*A cozy, magical adventure for all ages. A village with old warnings hiding in ordinary things. Thirteen ancient guardians. Two sixteen-year-olds. One dog with a lot of opinions.*

---

## How This Vault Works

Every document in this vault has a **status** — either on the whole document or on individual facts within it. Before reading anything, familiarize yourself with the three tiers:

- ✅ **CANON** — Locked in by player choices and session events. The world *is* this.
- 👁️ **REVEALED** — Players know this. Working truth. Not yet fully locked by choices.
- 🔒 **HIDDEN** — DM only. Can be revised freely until revealed.
