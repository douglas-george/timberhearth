# Canon Log

This is the living record of the Timberhearth campaign's most important moments: when things became true, and when hidden things came to light. Update this after every session.

---

## How to Use This Log

- **Promotions** record when something moved from Hidden → Revealed, or Revealed → Canon.
- **Locks** record new canon facts created directly by player choices (no prior hidden version).
- List the session number, what changed, and a brief note on how it happened.

---

## Session 01 — The Night of Voices

### Locks (New Canon)
- Gabriel Thatcher and Jessica Willowglen were called to the tower **together** — unprecedented in Timberhearth's history.
- Gabriel received a **dark crystal in the shape of a heart**, faintly luminous. Its power: to bring people together, once he gathers the pieces of himself first.
- Jessica received a **stone leaf**, lace-like and warm. Her power: the spark of nature — a seed waiting to grow.
- Gabriel's family gift: **65 coalmarks**.
- Jessica's family gift: **250 coalmarks**.
- Whistlewing asked them to walk the town, watch for strange signs, and return in seven days.

---

## Session 02 — The Stump and the Shadows

### Locks (New Canon)
- Gabriel bought a **lantern** from Ivan Ranger's General Store for **50 coalmarks**.
- Mossel struck a deal: clear the tree stump in the east meadow before returning to Whistlewing. Gabriel left 200 coalmarks as collateral.
- The stump was cleared. The promise was kept.
- Gabriel and Jessica visited **Shanna Parsnip**, the town's Wisdom, to learn about magic.
- They investigated strange animal tracks near the bakery.
- Glowing eyes in the alley — they fled the first time.

### Promotions
- Hidden → Revealed: Something unnatural is moving through Timberhearth at night.
- Hidden → Revealed: The Ringtailed Ringleader exists and is leading a synchronized group of raccoons.

---

## Session 03 — Secrets and Trust

### Locks (New Canon)
- In the alley, they chose to **follow the raccoons** rather than pursue the human figure on the rooftop.
- The Ringtailed Ringleader burned a **spiral sigil** into the alley wall. Jessica pressed bread to capture the mark.
- Maribel Willowglen was told about the raccoons — her reaction confirmed she already knows something.
- **Orrin Thatcher** revealed: he has been secretly working with the raccoons for weeks, causing minor mischief to draw the town's attention.
- The wild **pumpkin patch** was found at the edge of town — pumpkins glowing faintly, moving with unnatural intelligence.
- At the patch, Ringtail intervened with full magic power, drove back the blight, warned them *"the seal is failing"* — then **collapsed**.

### Promotions
- Hidden → Revealed: Orrin's connection to the raccoons and the Ringtailed Ringleader.
- Hidden → Revealed: A corrupted pumpkin patch exists outside town — the first sign of Vendraxis stirring.
- Hidden → Revealed: Ringtail is not just a raccoon. He wields real magic.
- Hidden → Revealed: *"The seal is failing."*

---

## Session 04 — The Circle of Thirteen

### Locks (New Canon)
- Whistlewing revealed: there are **thirteen Guardians** — thirteen former humans who surrendered their forms to bind Vendraxis. He is one of them.
- The players' gifts **resonate with the Guardians** and will draw them toward those they are meant to find.
- Gabriel and Jessica found and made contact with **Mira (Embermane)**, the fox Guardian, in her woodland shrine. She acknowledged them and set them a trial of balance, which they passed.
- Mira could not yet leave her post — the blight would spread if she abandoned it — but agreed to reconnect when the path clears.
- The **Tome of Hollowroot Fragment** (the full history of the Sealing) was encountered — the players now know the names and original human identities of all thirteen Guardians.

### Promotions
- Hidden → Revealed: Whistlewing = Elric Brightfeather, scholar-clockmaker.
- Hidden → Revealed: Ringtail = Kaelen of the Glades.
- Hidden → Revealed: Mira Cinderglen = Embermane the fox.
- Hidden → Revealed: All thirteen Guardian names (human and animal forms).
- Hidden → Canon: Whistlewing confirmed the seal is failing and that new champions are needed.

---

## Session 05 — The Mole, the Cat, and the Fray

### Locks (New Canon)
- Following Poodler into a tunnel below the village, Gabriel and Jessica found **Gnarltooth** (Garron Holt). He has been alone underground for centuries, holding back root-blight.
- Gnarltooth gave Poodler a **magical vest** that granted him speech for approximately 8 minutes. Poodler made the most of this.
- Gnarltooth expressed a desire to reconnect with Timberhearth. Asked them to bring a message back — a first step.
- Jessica's sign accident resulted in the Willowglen Bakery receiving every cat in Timberhearth.
- Among the cats was **Glowfern** — a silver cat, Guardian and Keeper of Poise. She set them three trials, which they passed, and gave Jessica a **silver whisker** as a gift.
- Glowfern has returned to the rooftops but is now aware of and connected to the champions.
- **The Great Pumpkin Fray**: Vendraxis launched a coordinated attack of Creeping Wither pumpkins through Timberhearth's streets. Gabriel, Jessica, and Poodler drove them back. The **town fountain was destroyed**.

### Promotions
- Hidden → Canon: Gnarltooth is beneath the town. His isolation is real and long.
- Hidden → Canon: Glowfern is active in Timberhearth, watching from the rooftops.
- Hidden → Revealed: Vendraxis can now reach inside the village. The seal is thin enough for direct incursion.
